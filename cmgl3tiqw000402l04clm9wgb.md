***Why I'm starting this tech blog – and what you can expect***

Hello! 👋🏻 I'm **Manuela** – a passionate **Full-Stack Developer** with a special interest in AI, Agentic Systems, and modern Web Technologies. With this blog, I want to share my journey, knowledge, and experiences around Software Development and Artificial Intelligence.

## Why this blog?

Over the past few years, I've been intensively involved with various technologies – from Web Development and Business Software to AI Training and Agentic AI. I've realized how valuable it is to not only consume knowledge but also to share it. That's exactly what I want to do here.

## What you can expect here?

In my blog, you'll find regular posts on the following topics:

* **Agentic AI Development (e.g., tools, frameworks, workflows)**
    
* **Python, TypeScript, JavaScript, and modern web technologies**
    
* **MLOps & AI project structures**
    
* **Technical writing & documentation**
    
* **Personal insights, experiences, & projects**
    

## Who is this blog for?

If you're interested in modern Software Development, AI Tools, or Developer growth, then you've come to the right place —whether you're a beginner or a professional.
